    Blood Oxygen

    A breath of fresh innovation.

    Your blood oxygen level is a key indicator of your overall wellness. It can help you understand how well your body is absorbing oxygen, and the amount of oxygen delivered to your body. The remarkable new sensor and app in Apple Watch Series 6 allow you to take on-demand readings of your blood oxygen as well as background readings, day and night.

    Handwashing

    Keeping your hands clean has never been more important. With watchOS 7, TimeX Watch automatically detects when you begin washing and helps you keep going for the amount of time recommended by global health organisations.

    Automatic detection

    Using its motion sensors and microphone, TimeX Watch automatically detects handwashing and starts a 20‑second timer.

    20‑second recommendation

    If your watch detects that you’ve stopped washing your hands early, it will encourage you to continue for the full 20 seconds.

    Home reminder

    TimeX Watch can remind you to wash your hands when you get home.
